About Norman Leonor...

Norman is a person who is a tech-savvy, curious about new digital technologies and aspires to become a Fulltime Full-Stack Web Developer in the near future. He's perceived his passion in computer programming way back in the early 90's when he got referred by a colleague for an employment in one of a local government unit in the Visayas Region and given the assignment in maintaining of the File Server stationed in the Mayor's office Electronics Data Processing Section and manages the overall operations of EDP section. Sometimes he will be required to made small modification of the code e.g. increasing the Mayor's Permit Taxes. Though with insufficient knowledge about networking, programming he was able to accomplished all the requirements on his own of the office. That's where he jumstart his passion in digital technologies and learned whatever a new techonology that's popular and widely talked about. He has learned some languages like C#, PHP, and Java and other technology like Wordpress and Laravel through self learning despite the fact that knowing big tech companies requires actual job experience, that he haven't acquired yet. He is determined to never give up as long his passion is still intense and he's happy of what he is doing in technology field he has not losen his faith that somehow somewhere the wheel of fortune will favor him eventually and succeed.
